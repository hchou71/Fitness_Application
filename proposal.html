Fitness Application Proposal

Problem Description

In current times where there is a strong sense of the importance of keeping a healthy lifestyle, we believe that people may not have the right resources to know how to begin. Our team would like to address the issues of people not knowing where to start working out, not having the knowledge of various exercises they can do, and not having a platform where they can track and share their progress. A majority of circulating fitness information today comes from fitness YouTubers and well-known fitness articles. Because of this, it is mostly the same general information with little variety. These influencers are all well into their fitness journeys, and their experiences with what works best might not necessarily work for everyone. We want gym goers and people that workout at home to be able to get advice and connect with others with the same needs. An example of an attempt to target our problem space would be the GymShark fitness mobile application. Although GymShark does provide various exercises to their users from certified trainers, there is no feature where users can interact with each other by sharing exercises they enjoyed. Additionally, GymShark only allows certified trainers to upload exercises, rather than users and trainers.

Proposed Application

Our app intends to target people who want or need suggestions on working out/fitness. They can be beginners or experienced. Trainers might be able to use this application to perform the schedule or introduce different exercises to their students.

Expected features include:

  • Users can upload their own exercise images with explanations as a way to get beginners started.
      -Users can also comment under these exercise posts.
      -Users can also save user posted workouts to view later.
  • Search and filter functionality so users can search for exercises for the muscle group they want to target that day.
      -We are considering it being a map of a human body that users can select different muscles they want to target.
  • Online forum where people can post fitness questions and start online discussions of various topics. Similar to a reddit page but specifically for health and fitness tips and discussions.
  • Users can log their progress based on the number of sets and repetitions they complete and the weight they lifted.
      -Users will also be able to view their past workout logs to keep track of different workout progressions.

We will be using this csv from kaggle in order to organize exercises by the muscles that are being utilized.

We will also want to use this api on fitness exercises if approved by Joel.

Our platform will provide users with a feature to share their experiences so that we can foster a healthy environment where users of any experience level can find and share answers with each other. Our platform also provides preset workouts to view and try out, while also providing them with images or videos to show how to approach various exercises. Additionally, our platform will also provide users a function to look back at their previous workouts and to look at saved exercises they wanted to do at a later time. We believe that our platform will allow users to positively approach working out, regardless of their experience level, because we are providing a platform for health discussions amongst users, find new exercises they can try, and save and share various exercises they enjoy.
